🍗 Chicken Biryani

This is a simple and tasty recipe for Indian Chicken Biryani.

Ingredients:

Marinating Chicken

  • Chicken – 500g (with bone, cut into medium pieces)
  • Yogurt – 1/2 cup
  • Ginger-garlic paste – 1½ tbsp
  • Red chili powder – 1½ tsp
  • Turmeric – ½ tsp
  • Garam masala – 1 tsp
  • Lemon juice – 1 tbsp
  • Salt – to taste
  • Mint leaves – ½ cup (chopped)
  • Coriander leaves – ½ cup (chopped)
  • Green chilies – 2 (slit)

Rice

  • Basmati rice – 2 cups
  • Whole spices – 1 bay leaf, 4 cloves, 4 green cardamoms, 1-inch cinnamon
  • Salt – to taste

🔪 Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken (Minimum 1 hour, overnight best):
    • Mix all marination ingredients with the chicken.
    • Cover and refrigerate.
  2. Fry the Onions:
    • Heat oil in a pan, fry sliced onions till golden and crisp.
    • Remove and set aside on paper towels.
  3. Cook the Rice (70–80% cooked):
    • Soak rice for 30 minutes.
    • Boil water with whole spices and salt.
    • Add rice, cook until 70–80% done.
    • Drain and set aside.
  4. Layering:
    • In a heavy-bottomed pot (or pressure cooker without the whistle), add marinated chicken (spread at the bottom).
    • Layer ½ of the fried onions, mint, and coriander leaves.
    • Add ½ the rice, then repeat layers.
    • Sprinkle saffron milk and ghee on top.
  5. Dum Cooking (Steam Method):
    • Cover the lid tightly. You can seal edges with dough or use a heavy lid.
    • Cook on low flame for 30–35 minutes (or 15 minutes on medium, then 15 minutes on low).
    • Alternatively, place a tawa under the pot and cook on low flame to avoid burning.
  6. Serve:
    • Let it rest for 10 minutes after turning off the heat.
    • Gently mix and serve with raita, boiled eggs, or salan.
