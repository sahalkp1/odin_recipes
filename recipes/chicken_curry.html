Chicken Curry

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry well.
  3. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Mix in chili, coriander, and turmeric powder.
  5. Add chicken pieces and cook until tender.
  6. Season with salt and serve hot.
