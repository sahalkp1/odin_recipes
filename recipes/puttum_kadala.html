Puttu and Kadala Curry

Ingredients

  • 1 cup roasted rice flour
  • ½ cup grated coconut
  • 1 cup black chickpeas (kadala), soaked overnight
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Steam puttu by layering rice flour and coconut in a puttu maker.
  2. Pressure cook kadala until soft.
  3. Sauté onions, add tomatoes and spices, then mix in kadala.
  4. Cook well until gravy thickens.
  5. Serve hot with steamed puttu.
